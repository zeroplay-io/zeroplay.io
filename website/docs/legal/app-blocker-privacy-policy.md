# App Blocker Privacy Policy

App Blocker is designed to keep your Screen Time choices private and on your device.

## Data Collection

App Blocker does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party crash-reporting services in the app.

The app does not create an account and does not require your name, email address, phone number, or location.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is processed and stored locally so the app can provide its features:

- Apps, categories, and websites selected through Apple's Screen Time controls
- Focus, hiding, website-blocking, and scheduling settings
- App preferences and subscription entitlement status provided by the App Store
- App-lock credentials, stored as protected cryptographic hashes in the iOS Keychain

This information is not sent to ZeroPlay servers. Screen Time selections are represented by opaque identifiers managed by Apple; App Blocker cannot inspect the user's private app-usage history.

## Screen Time Permission

App Blocker uses Apple's Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Device Activity frameworks only after the user grants Screen Time permission. These frameworks are used to apply restrictions selected by the user. Permission can be revoked in iOS Settings.

## Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. ZeroPlay does not receive or store payment-card details. Apple may process purchase information under Apple's own privacy policy.

## Optional Support Requests

If you choose to contact us or submit feedback, we receive only the information you voluntarily provide so we can respond to the request. This support interaction is optional, separate from the app's core functionality, and is not used for advertising or tracking.

## Children's Privacy

Because App Blocker does not collect personal data through the app, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy when App Blocker's functionality or legal requirements change. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact [support@zeroplay.io](mailto:support@zeroplay.io).
